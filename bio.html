Wanda Von Bremen is a photographer and visual artist focused in environmental issues and nature destruction from Berlin, Germany.
In 2018, she received a Bachelor of Science in Landscape Management and Nature Protection at the University of Sustainable Development. During 2013 and 2014, she worked as an Ecology Researcher at Osa Conservation in Costa Rica, where she communicated about wildlife projects amongst the country.

In 2015, she worked as a Biology Field Assistant in the field station Kirindy Forest in Madagascar, implementing conservation programs of local species.
In 2017, she relocated to Chile to continue working as an environmental investigator for the University of Christian Humanist. She has exhibited her work in “The Perfect Image” show at The Local NY Gallery in New York in 2019 and shot a campaign for the Generation Foundation (Generationen-Stiftung) in Berlin in 2018.
She is currently enrolled in the New Media Narrative program at the International Center of Photography in New York.
