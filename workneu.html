Patagonia

You took my breath away with the strong wind you blow.
You crashed into my heart without even letting me prepare for it.
You created your own little ice cave in my soul, so cold and blue I freeze whenever I think about you.
You showed me how life can be and how I want to live.
You made me discover my limits and showed me that going on your own isn’t that hard anymore.
You made me find people with whom I immediately had a familiar connection.
Thank you for this amazing time we had together. It was short but is was enough to fall in love with you.
You will always be a part of me.
