Home Work Book: Holisis Bio Contact

“Holisis” is a handmade-book of color photographs, scans, and collages. The visual imagery gives space to the concept of interdependence. Emphasizing the diversity of nature, the work aims to dispel negative perceptions of earth’s ignored or unseen elements. Every organism in the life-cycle is irreplaceable and creates a delicate balance that needs to be maintained. In exploring this idea of circuition, some pages are self-made and may contain germinated seeds that will grow and die. As a result, the book will be different every time it is read.
